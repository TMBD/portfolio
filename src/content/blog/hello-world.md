Welcome! This is the first post on my new portfolio blog. I built this site to
share what I'm learning as a software engineer — the ideas, the tools, and the
occasional hard-won lesson.

## What to expect

I'll write about things like:

- **Engineering** — architecture, testing, and writing code that lasts.
- **Web development** — performance, accessibility, and good UX.
- **Tools & workflow** — the setup that keeps me productive.

## Why a blog?

Writing forces clarity. Explaining an idea in plain words is the fastest way I
know to find the gaps in my own understanding — and hopefully some of it is
useful to you too.

Thanks for reading. More soon!
